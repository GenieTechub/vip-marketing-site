About Vivasaayipulla Angadi

Welcome to Vivasaayipulla Angadi – The Future of Agriculture Marketplace and the World's First Voice-Based Ecommerce platform. We connect people, products, and services in one place – easy, affordable, and trustworthy. Our mission is to make agriculture products and services accessible to everyone.

We provide a platform where anyone can sell or buy products directly without middlemen. As the world's first voice-based ecommerce platform, we offer powerful features like voice search, live chat, and multilingual support, making farming smarter and business easier. Buy and sell all kinds of agriculture products directly from farmers, sellers, and local shops.
